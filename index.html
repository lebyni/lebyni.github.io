BRIEF BIOGRAPHY

Currently I am a final-year PhD candidate at Peking University under the superivision of Prof. Yao Guo and Prof. Xiangqun Chen. I previously interned in MSRA working with Senior Researcher Yuanchun Li and Principle Researcher Yunxin Liu.

My research interests include Internet-of-Things, network computing and operating system. I am a co-recipient of the best paper award of IEEE ICC'19 and IEEE IoP'18, and the outstanding paper award of IEEE HPCC'19. I currently serves/have served as an associate editor for IEEE Transactions on Vehicular Technology and Peer-to-Peer Networking and Applications, a guest editor for IEEE Wireless Communications, IEEE Transactions on Industrial Informatics, IEEE Network and CCF Transactions on Networking, and a TPC member of many international conferences, including IEEE INFOCOM, Globecom, WCNC, etc. I also served as the General Co-Chair for IEEE BigDataSE'20, TPC Co-Chair for IEEE BigDataSE'19, a poster co-chair for IEEE MASS’18, a track co-chair for IEEE/CIC ICCC’19, IEEE I-SPAN’18 and VTC’17 Fall, and an active reviewer for over 20 international journals. I am a member of IEEE and ACM. I was recognized as a highly cited researcher by Clarivate.
