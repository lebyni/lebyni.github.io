BRIEF BIOGRAPHY

Currently I am a final-year PhD candidate at Peking University under the superivision of Prof. Yao Guo and Prof. Xiangqun Chen. I previously interned in MSRA working with Senior Researcher Yuanchun Li and Principle Researcher Yunxin Liu. I work on intelligent mobile/edge computing, which covers a large range of research topics including mobile/edge computing, federated learning, model compression and transfer learning. Specifically, my goal is to make DNNs efficient,customized and secure for mobile/edge devices.

  • Efficiency: inference efficiency (ACMMM’19); data efficiency (AAAI’21).
  • Customization: cloud-to-edge model customization framework (Ubicomp’21).
  • Security: privacy-preserving federated personalization framework (WWW’21, Ubicomp’22).
